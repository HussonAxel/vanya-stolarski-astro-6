    ## Design Context

### Users
First-time visitors discovering the author and illustrator's world. Their main job is to quickly understand who she is, what they can explore, and where to go next without friction.

### Brand Personality
Refined, literary, warm.

### Aesthetic Direction
Refined editorial. Premium but restrained. Strong typography, elegant spacing, warm paper-like surfaces, dark forest accents, subtle art-book atmosphere. Avoid trendy SaaS card grids, overly decorative gradients, or hover-first interaction design.

### Design Principles
- Discovery should feel guided, not crowded.
- Typography and spacing carry the hierarchy more than effects do.
- Every interactive element must remain legible and obvious without hover.
- Mobile gets a rethought composition, not a shrunken desktop mosaic.
- Decorative detail should support the author and illustrator identity, not compete with the content.
